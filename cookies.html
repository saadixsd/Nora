Cookies Policy

Understanding Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit websites. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, as well as to provide information to the website owners.

How We Use Cookies

At XenoraAI, we use cookies for several purposes:

  • Essential Operation: Cookies necessary for our website to function properly
  • Performance: Cookies that help us understand how visitors interact with our site
  • Functionality: Cookies that remember your preferences
  • Analytics: Cookies that help us improve our services

Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Session Cookies Essential for site navigation and maintaining your session Until you close your browser
Preference Cookies Remember your language and display preferences 1 year
Security Cookies Protect against cross-site request forgery and other attacks 24 hours
Analytics Cookies Help us understand how visitors use our site to improve it 2 years

Third-Party Cookies

We may use services from trusted third parties that set their own cookies. These include:

  • Google Analytics: For understanding website traffic and usage patterns
  • Hotjar: For improving user experience through heatmaps and session recordings
  • LinkedIn: For our social media integration

These third-party services have their own privacy policies, and we recommend you review them.

Managing Cookies

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish. Most web browsers allow some control of cookies through browser settings. You can:

  • Delete all cookies from your device
  • Set your browser to prevent cookies being placed
  • Use browser add-ons to manage specific cookies

To learn more about how to manage cookies, visit allaboutcookies.org.
